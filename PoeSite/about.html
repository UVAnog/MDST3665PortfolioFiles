About Poe

Hi, I'm Poe the cat!

You may know me or have seen me before, but if not, here's my story!
I was found on the side of a busy road in Richmond, Virginia. I was only a few weeks old when I was found, I was very scared. A few college kids in Charlottesville though told the kind person who found me they would love to have me and take care of me. So, as of now, I'm chilling with my college homies at the University of Virginia. They give me lots of love and attention and in return I'll snuggle, or give bites.
